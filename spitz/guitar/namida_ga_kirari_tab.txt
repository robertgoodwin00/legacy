To be true to the original song, only strum the lower notes of those chords


Here is the order:

Intro(Intro End) x 2 -> Verse(Ending 1) -> Verse(Ending 2) ->
Bridge(Ending 1) -> Bridge(Ending 2) -> Chorus x 2 -> Chorus Cap -> 
Intro(Intro End) x 2 -> Verse(Ending 1) -> Verse(with Bass Slide and Ending 2) ->
Bridge(Ending 1) -> Bridge(Ending 2) -> Chorus x 2 -> Chorus Cap(with A7) ->
Chorus x 2 -> Chorus Cap -> Intro(Intro End) -> Intro(with Intro End Final)


Going through the chorus at the end of the song the bass does
some interesting things that I left out because it'd be too difficult
to synchronize them with the lead guitar part.
