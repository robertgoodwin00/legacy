
Hechima no Hana ("The Loofah Flower")
by Spitz
from the album "Sora no Tobikata"
transcribed by Robert Goodwin


This is a very pretty song consisting of arpeggios.  The 5th
and 7th frets are barred for many of them.  There might be
easier ways to play this song.  This way is doable atleast.
